# South Tampa

South Tampa is one of the most desirable neighborhoods in the Tampa Bay area, known for its perfect blend of urban convenience and residential charm. With a Walk Score of 68, many daily errands can be accomplished on foot.

## Lifestyle & Vibe

If you've ever been to Chicago's Lincoln Park or Portland's Pearl District, you'll feel right at home in South Tampa. The neighborhood features:

- **Tree-lined streets** with Spanish moss-draped oaks
- **Boutique fitness studios** - yoga, Pilates, cycling, barre
- **Brunch culture** - Wait times on Sunday mornings are a badge of honor
- **Local coffee shops** that know your order by heart
- **Dog-friendly everything** - Parks, patios, and walking paths

## Popular Sub-Areas

### Hyde Park
The crown jewel of South Tampa, Hyde Park offers:
- High-end shopping and dining on Hyde Park Village
- Historic bungalows and Mediterranean Revival architecture
- Top-rated schools
- Median home price: $750,000+

### Sunset Park
Family-oriented community with:
- Tree-canopied streets
- Neighborhood schools within walking distance
- Community pool and recreation
- Median home price: $550,000

### Beach Park
Near Bayshore Boulevard with:
- Waterfront views and parks
- Running/cycling along the bay
- Mix of historic and modern homes
- Median home price: $650,000

## Amenities & Attractions

- **Restaurants**: From casual tacos to upscale steakhouses
- **Shopping**: Hyde Park Village, SoHo district
- **Parks**: Bayshore Boulevard (longest continuous sidewalk in the US), Ballast Point Park
- **Schools**: Top-rated public and private schools
- **Nightlife**: Wine bars, craft cocktail lounges, live music venues
- **Beaches**: 15-20 minutes to Clearwater and St. Pete beaches

## Real Estate Market

South Tampa commands premium prices due to high demand and limited inventory:

- **Single-family homes**: $450K - $2M+
- **Condos/Townhomes**: $300K - $800K
- **Average days on market**: 15-25 days
- **Market characteristics**: Multiple offers common, move-in ready homes sell fast

## Who Lives Here?

South Tampa attracts:
- Young professionals working downtown
- Growing families seeking top schools
- Empty nesters downsizing but staying central
- Transplants from Chicago, New York, Boston

## Considerations

- **Traffic**: Rush hour congestion on main arteries
- **Parking**: Can be tight in Hyde Park Village area
- **Flooding**: Some areas prone to tidal flooding (check flood zones)
- **Competition**: Expect multiple offers on desirable properties
